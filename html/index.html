Protocol

How we work.

Access before the camera.

Every production starts with context reading, presence, and alignment with the territory. Before any image comes the relationship. Before any rush comes understanding.

Usage, window, territories, formats, and editorial restrictions are defined in the contract. We reduce risk, avoid ambiguities, and protect all parties involved.

One point of contact for each phase.

Patricia leads the briefing, commercial interface, and contract coordination. Elias leads the field, direction, and territorial execution. The client always knows who they are speaking with and what stage the project is at.

Aesthetics with responsibility.

We don't pursue images without context. We seek visually strong, culturally sensitive, and technically solid narratives.
